Dumpling Recipe

Dumplings are small pieces of dough, originating from various parts of the world, typically wrapped around a filling. Whether you prefer them sweet or salty, Dumplings are the perfect flexible food to experiment with flavor!

Ingredients:

    2 cups of flours 1/2 teaspoon of salt 4 teaspoons of butter 3/4 cup of milk

Steps:

  1. Mix and Sift dry ingredients
  2. Begin to work in the butter with the tips of your fingers
  3. Add milk gradually, using a knife to mix
  4. Toss mixture onto a floured board
  5. Pat and roll out one half inch in thickness
  6. Place close together in a buttered steamer
  7. Put over kettle of boiling water, cover closed and steam for 12 mins
  9. When finished, you may take spoonfuls and drop and cook on top of stew
  10. In the case of some liquid needing to be removed, dumplings may rest on meat and potato and not settle into liquid
